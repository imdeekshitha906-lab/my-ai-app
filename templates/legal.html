Your privacy — the plain truth

  • We do not sell, share, or give your data to anyone.
  • We do not run ads and do not use your conversations for advertising.
  • Your intake details, chats, and partner-notes live in your own browser's storage on your device — not in our database. Clearing your browser data erases them.
  • Incognito chats auto-erase from your device 72 hours after creation, and never appear in your regular chat list.
  • To generate replies, your messages are processed by Google's Gemini API (our AI engine). That processing is governed by Google's API terms. Please avoid sharing information that could identify third parties (full names, phone numbers, addresses).
  • Feedback you send is anonymous and stored only to help us improve {{ app_name }}'s guidance — never your chat content itself.
  • You may use {{ app_name }} without creating any account.
